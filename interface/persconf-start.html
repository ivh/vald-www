In the form below you can change the selection of linelists and the weights that are being used when extracting from the VALD database. The higher the weight, the more emphasis is given on that aspect during extraction. It is also possible to disable some of the linelists.

Any linelists that were recently added to VALD will automatically appear in your personal configuration file below. However, these linelists will be listed as inactive. Similarly, linelists that no longer are part of VALD will automatically be removed for your personal configuration.
